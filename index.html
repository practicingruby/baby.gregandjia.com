A welcoming party for Leo Brown!

Our brand new baby boy will be making his way into the world in mid-May. We plan to celebrate this joyful occasion the only way we know how, with an awesome party for our friends and family.

RSVP

We hope that as many of our friends and family can come to this event as possible, but we know that people have very busy schedules and may not be able to make it. To help us plan the party, please fill out this RSVP form if you plan to attend.

Gifts

While it may be a break from tradition, we'd prefer if you didn't give us any sort of costly gifts for this baby shower. In fact, we'd be perfectly happy for you to show up empty handed!

However, if you'd like to do something nice for Leo, you can get him a baby book. Please take a look at our recommendations if this sounds like something you'd like to do.

Used Baby Stuff

We don't want people to go out and spend their money on new stuff for us, but we're open to accepting pre-owned stuff that folks are no longer making use of. If you have things that you want to give to us, please email jiawu.jw@gmail.com and we'll get back to you.

Save the Date

The party will be at our house on April 15 from 12pm to 4pm.

We'll provide a light lunch and some drinks, but feel free to bring your own drinks as well.
